When I finished my degree in Information Technology I applied and got hired as Android programming Professor at the same time I took Master in Information Technology when I graduated, I worked as a freelance android developer and started to accept projects I also try to share my knowledge to others by speaking at conferences.

As time goes by, I have done more project from different companies but that didn’t stop me to learn more I’m still trying and determined to learn new technologies and skills.

Currently, I’m focused on moving to the next stage of my career and achieving mid-level developer status.
